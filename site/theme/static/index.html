Blockchain revolutionises securities

Blockchain technology changes the way digital assets can be traded globally. Soon securities can be issued and sold quickly, cost effectively, and with unprecedented transparency. Small and medium enterprises can go public by issuing stocks and bonds in a compliant manner, just like the large corporations.

What we do

Crypto Securities Exchange is a trading platform where digital stocks, bonds and other digital securities can be traded regardless of the issuing party. On our platform you can buy, sell and research securities with easy access. You can start trading in minutes.

Liquidity like never before

Our focus is on crowdfunded equity, small and medium enterprise shares and debt as well as peer-to-peer investing instruments. These assets will benefit most from new blockchain technology driven opportunities.

Novice friendly

Information flow

Fully Compliant

How blockchain technology works

The first blockchain technology was Bitcoin, a digital currency and peer-to-peer payment system created in 2010. Now the technology has matured and is ready for applications beyond payments. Bitcoin and other emerging blockchains allow one to provably, and instantly transfer ownership of any digital asset without a third party settlement. A new class of trade settlement tools can leverage these transparent, democratic principles. Historically such power has only been open to, and developed by, large financial institutions.
